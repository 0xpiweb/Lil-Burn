# 🎨 Lil-Burn: Community 1/1 Integrations

This folder documents the high-fidelity 1/1 assets integrated into the Lil-Burn collection following the **"Vision of Burn"** community art event in February 2026. Out of 25 open applications, these 5 works were selected by community vote and team review for official protocol integration.

## ⚙️ The Auction Mechanic
Starting **March 16, 2026**, these 1/1s enter a weekly auction cycle on secondary marketplaces. 
**100% of auction proceeds** are committed to the **Weekly Epoch Buyback Budget**, directly fueling the manual floor sweeps and permanent supply deflation.
